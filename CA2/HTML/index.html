-- Facts about SP --


# Polytechnic Year Founded
1 Singapore Poly 1954
2 Ngee Ann Poly 1963
3 Temasek Poly 1990
4 Nanyang Poly 1992
5 Republic Poly 2002

SP is Singapore's first polytechnic established in 1954. It has a rich history and has set the standard for top-tier education.

SP was among the first in the world to implement flipped learning at an institutional level.

SP alumnus is Singapore's largest poly alumni. It also allows students to gain first-hand exposure to industry leading companies.

SP is the only polytechnic to be connected directly to a MRT station (Dover MRT). It also offers a variety of food from its 6 foodcourts.

SP offers over a hundred CCA clubs to choose from. It has a variety of sports, arts, service learning, special interests and constituent clubs for students to pick.

SP is the first polytechnic to introduce a fully customised elective programme. It offers over a hundred electives for students to indulge so that they can fuel their thirst for knowledge and deepen thier expertise.

Did you know?


SP was first established in 1954 and was initially located at Prince Edward Road. The official opening at this location was conducted by Prince Philip, the Duke of Edinburgh, on February 24, 1959!

In 1970, a temporary additional campus site was acquired at Ayer Rajah Road.

Finally in 1971, our current campus site at Dover was chosen as the permanant home for SP.

In 1972, when Ayer Rajah Road campus was completed, SP had 3 functioning campuses.
